Our Little Story


Janatara Cafe & Eatery is open in Bandung near Asia Afrika area. If you love a great coffee, we provide different kinds of beans for manual brew
We have food choice, including wholewheat bread or waffles.
Our food offering include a wide variety of pastas, indonesian food like ayam geprek
different tasty flavours of fried rice , and plenty western foods to choose. And never forget the mouthwatering dessert, including our soft waffles with ice cream, banana tower and many more.
Janatara Cafe & Eatery was opened on the last June 2022 and has provided us with real experience to make sure our cafe & eatery deliver best taste for our customer including food & coffee experience.
